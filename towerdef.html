Tower Defense

A partnered game based in HTML, Java and CSS. Try to shoot all the enemies that try to invade your castle. (Oct 2021)

Tower Defense is a game that my partner and I worked on for about two weeks to create from scratch. There was some difficulty getting started but after researching and receiving feedback, we were able to overcome our difficulties for our game. What I worked on for this project was programming and publishing, while my partner worked on the art and sound for the game. Our game is mainly written in Java and most mechanics are based on the original tower defense game that we used to play as kids. Give it a shot, our game is right below.

You can find the link to Tower Defense here.

The goal of the game is defend your tower from all of the enemies that are coming in waves. When the game continues, the mobs get faster but also gives more points, however once an enemy reaches your tower, the game is over.

This game was made in 2021 but if I were to make any changes to it now, I'd add weapon upgrade mechanics, mob art and different enemy possiblities.
