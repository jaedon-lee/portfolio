Crossy Rockets

A solo project revamping the classic Rocket Patrol into my own version. (July 2020)

Crossy Rockets was the first game that I ever built/reworked and I had a lot of fun with it. I chose my own art, background, sound, etc, in order to achieve the right aesthetic that I was looking for in my rework. At the start of the game, the player is prompted with two difficulties, easy or hard; easy is slower and also more health, while hard has less health and the enemies move dramatically faster.

I borrowed some code from the original game but changed the mechanics and dynamics of the game into something like Crossy Roads. The player tries to survive the endless rocket spaceships that are flying in from the right side of the screen and if the player survives for 60 seconds then they win.

If the player runs out of HP, they lose.
