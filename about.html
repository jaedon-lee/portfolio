About Me

Hi, I'm Jaedon and I just graduated from UCSC with a degree in Game Design. I had a heavy focus on programming and front end development for games, web applications and mobile apps. I also work on the design side of game development such as 3D modeling, shaders and animation. I've used these skills to create different projects and games, ones that I've worked on individually and others that I've worked on with teams. My adaptability to new environments has contributed to many of the learning experiences in my life and positively impacted my learning curve for skills, responsibilities and positions. As a previous Vice President External for Lambda Phi Epsilon, I coordinated over twenty special events between different organizations across the country in order to further build our networking connections among people in the same demographic. After graduating, I'm looking for a job in game design, front-end development or graphic design.
